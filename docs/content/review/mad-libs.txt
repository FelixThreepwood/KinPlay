# KinPlay Mad Libs

## Moon Pancake Mission
- ID: `moon_pancake_mission`
- Type: mad_libs
- Modes: mad_libs
- Age: 4–8
- Duration: 8 min
- Energy: calm
- Summary: A silly space breakfast story.
- Materials: none
- Safety tags: quiet, no_materials, reading_help, sibling_friendly
- Fields: A name (person_name, e.g. Zigzag), An adjective (adjective, e.g. wiggly), A food (food, e.g. pancakes), An animal (animal, e.g. llama), A funny sound (sound, e.g. boing)
- Template: Captain {name} flew a {adjective} rocket to the moon to deliver {food}. When the hatch opened, a moon {animal} shouted {sound}! Everyone laughed and shared breakfast among the stars.
- Read-aloud note: Read with a dramatic countdown.
