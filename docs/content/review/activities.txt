# KinPlay Activities

## Animal Mirror Parade
- ID: `animal_mirror_parade`
- Type: activity
- Modes: quick_play, pick_a_game
- Age: 2–8
- Duration: 8 min
- Energy: medium
- Summary: Take turns leading silly animal movements while everyone mirrors the leader.
- Materials: none
- Safety tags: parent_supervision, movement, no_materials, sibling_friendly
- Setup: Clear a small safe space for walking and wiggling.
- Play steps: Choose one leader. / The leader moves like an animal while everyone copies. / Switch leaders after three animals.
- Parent note: Use gentle movements for younger children and avoid jumping near furniture.
- Variations: Whisper the animal for a quiet round. / Let each child invent a brand-new animal.
